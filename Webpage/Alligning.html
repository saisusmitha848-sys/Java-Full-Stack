Why Alt Text Matters

Alt text provides a text alternative to images for screen reader users, search engines, and situations where the image cannot be loaded.

Good alt text is concise, descriptive, and helps anyone who cannot see the picture understand its purpose.

For this image, the alt text focuses on the subject and the scene: a baby boy playing outside and smiling.

Notes
